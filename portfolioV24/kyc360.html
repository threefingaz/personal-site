KYC360: One-Stop KYC Management System for Multinational Financial Organization

Disclaimer: The project description is based on actual work experience. However, for confidentiality and legal reasons, all visuals and specific details included in this presentation are conceptual and have been modified. Any resemblance to actual events or living people is purely coincidental.

Role

My role was to analyze complex business processes and devise comprehensive solutions. My responsibilities ranged from understanding the basic requirements—such as initiating or terminating corporate client relationships—to managing detailed KYC reviews requiring coordination both internally and with client representatives.

Project Scope

  • Internal System: Designed for KYC operations within the organization, facilitating coordination among various business and IT groups.
  • External Interface: Developed for corporate clients, enhancing their engagement through a user-friendly platform.

System Design

Internal Tools:

Simplified user interface that hides underlying complexities, with distinct pipelines for different processes ensuring continuous system communication.

User Roles:

Sales Personnel:

Access performance metrics and receive alerts on KYC issues or client-related actions.

Client Relationship Managers:

Handle KYC reviews and maintain accountability for client interactions, with direct access to forthcoming KYC events and crucial updates.

Client Page:

Summarizes all available data for individual clients, enhancing transparency and accessibility.

Business Support Operations:

Manage daily operational tasks, resolve KYC issues, and support overall business processes.

Search Functionality: Advanced search capabilities using common identifiers or free text, ensuring precise client matching.

Bulk Operations: Perform multiple operations on selected results, with access to key KYC and performance metrics.

Dashboard: An actionable starting screen that provides a comprehensive overview of ongoing processes and highlights areas requiring attention.

Challenges and Solutions

  • Standardization: Addressed the need for standardized processes across global locations to reduce KYC risks and associated fines. Transparency and Automation: Integrated a single source of truth for KYC data, improving transparency and reducing manual interventions through structured communications.

Team Collaboration

Conducted regular catch-ups with business users to refine requirements and develop the solution.
Employed Figma prototypes for external user testing to gather feedback and ensure the solution's effectiveness from the outset.

Worked alongside two development teams: a local team of 10 members and a remote team. For the client-accessible part, I worked together with the product owner and design department of the client experience team.
